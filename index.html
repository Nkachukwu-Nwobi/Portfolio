Skool LMS

This project was built to:

  • Help prospective students seamlessly apply to Decagon University and track their application progress
  • Aid Decagon admissions staff in managing the applications i.e accept or reject applications.

Skool LMS is an application portal that allows prospective students of the Decagon University to register, sign in and apply for their preferred course, monitor their application progress and communicate with the admissions staff to get tips and help with the process.

The portal also allows the admissions staff to sign in, manage the applications i.e accept or reject applications and communicate with the applicants. The admissions staff can also and also download or delete applications

Role: Team lead, Full-stack development, including frontend and backend implementation.
Technologies Used: React.js, Redux, Javascript, Typescript, ExpressJS, sqlite, Docker, Socket.io, Tailwind CSS

This project was built to solve two problems

  • The delay in getting results post examination in most Nigerian universities.

The application also includes an admin functionality that enables lecturers, register, signin to the platform to manage their departmental courses and exams. Lecturers are able to set exams and administer them on the application.
